Code Reproducibility Training

Training material to help you create FAIR (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable and Reusable) code and scripts.


What we do

We aim to create training materials that help learners acquire the core skills required to develop sustainable and reproducible code. We do so by creating educational content and training targeted for an audience with non-computing backgrounds, using hands-on examples that are developed further in each subsequent lesson creating a learning path for users to follow. This enables us to share best practices in coding in a structured and accessible way, allowing others to also use tools and practices that were previously limited to the computational-savvy.


Who we are

We are a community from across the world who work in ELIXIR-Europe, and academic and industrial organisations. We share a common passion in education and FAIR software practices, and we advocate these tirelessly. Check out our existing contributors!
